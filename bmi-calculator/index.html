BMI Calculator

Calculate your body mass index quickly with CalcHive's BMI Calculator. Enter weight and height (metric or imperial) to get an instant BMI estimate and category.

CalcHive runs the calculator in your browser — inputs are not stored and no account is required.

What this calculator helps with

This BMI Calculator provides a quick estimate of body mass index, useful for screening and basic tracking. It is not a medical diagnostic tool.

BMI Calculator FAQ

What does the BMI Calculator result mean?
BMI gives a rough estimate of body fat based on height and weight. Use it as a screening tool, not a diagnosis.
Are my inputs stored?
No. Inputs are processed in your browser and are not sent to our servers.
Which units are supported?
Both metric and imperial units are supported; select the unit system you prefer when entering values.
Is the BMI Calculator free to use?
Yes — the BMI Calculator is free and requires no signup.
